How to use?

1. Upload the covid19 proof such as medical documents etc on the upload document link at top-left.

2. Enter the details in "Enter new(update) state/city/country ". Provide correct patient_id ( Aadhar card), Doctor_id, hash of file uploaded in step 1.

3. Your entry will be added to the blockchain. You can also see the covid details of any state/city/country, see all covid19 patients records for that place and can also delete any state/city/country.

NOTE-: The state/city/country name spelling should be according to https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lists_of_cities.
